AI RESUME SCREENING

Our AI-powered resume screening scores your resume on key criteria. Get actionable steps to revamp your resume and land more interviews. The website goes beyond checking and uses leading artificial intelligence technology to grade the resume on 100+ resume checks at a time. Specifically, the platform analyzes the resume's impact by evaluating the strength of your word choice and also checks the resume's style and brevity.

Similarly, it also scores each of the bullet points on the resume and checks for key elements such as inconsistencies, length, word choice, filter words, keywords, and buzzwords.

Our online resume grader provides better resume feedback than most so-called 'professional' reviewers out there, who often give outdated, wrong, or non-actionable advice and charge hundreds of dollars. By using artificial intelligence, the resumes are evaluated and ranked according to the criteria given by HR. It is a very effective idea to overcome the manual screening of freshers.

The AI Resume screening allows you to sort the CV's based on the similarity between the job description The higher the similarity score, the better the CV matches the job description.

The candidates are shortlisted based on their CV scores.


The Integrated email functionality is used to send notifications to the shortlisted candidates about the next steps in the hiring process.

It maintains continuous updates with the HR team, providing relevant information throughout the hiring process.
